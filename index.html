Our Team


Daniel: Team Principal

Daniel is our Team Principal. His job is to coordinate our efforts at Cobra Racing and make sure that everything goes smoothly.

Charlie: Design Engineer & Graphic Designer

Charlie is our Design Engineer. He is the mastermind behind our National Finals car, the NSP-1. He is also our Graphic Designer, and he designed our logo and our colour scheme.

Craig: Head of Enterprise

As our Head of Enterprise, Craig manages both our money and marketing. He acquires our sponsorships and he created our website.

Padraig: Co-head of Design and Enterprise

Padraig is always looking out for ways to improve the NSP-2 is collaboration with Charlie. He also works with Craig to manage our finances and sponsors.

About Us. Who we are and how we got here.

We're an F1 in Schools team from Naas Community College in County Kildare, Ireland. We are all 2nd years and it's our second time participating in this competition. Last year, we won the Judges' Choice award at the National Finals. Read our blog post to learn more.
